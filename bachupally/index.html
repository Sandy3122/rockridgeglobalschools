Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions from Parents

Find answers to the most common questions about our programs and enrollment process.

What age groups do you accept?
We currently accept children from 2 to 5 years, grouped by age and developmental stage.
What are your safety and hygiene practices?
Daily cleaning, frequent sanitisation of high-touch areas, secure entry/exit, background-checked staff and regular safety drills.
Do you provide snacks or meals?
We offer a healthy snack option and have a clear food policy. Parents can also send food based on their child's needs.
How do I start the admission process?
Simply fill in the enquiry form below or call us. Our team will help you with a tour, fee structure and enrollment steps.
What are your operating hours?
Our preschool operates from Monday to Friday, 8:30 AM to 1:00 PM. Extended hours may be available upon request.
Do you offer transportation services?
Transportation services may be available depending on the location. Please contact us for more details about transportation options.
